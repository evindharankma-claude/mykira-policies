# KWSP / EPF Projection Calculator

[← Back to MYkira](./)

Project your Kumpulan Wang Simpanan Pekerja (KWSP / Employees Provident Fund) balance at retirement.

## What it computes

Given:
- **Current EPF balance**
- - **Monthly salary** (employee + employer contribution rates apply automatically)
  - - **Years to retirement**
    - - **Annual dividend rate** (default 6.0% — historically KWSP has paid 5.0–6.5%; editable)
      - - **Optional i-Saraan voluntary contributions** (capped at **RM 100,000 per calendar year** under the EPF Act)
       
        - …MYkira computes your projected balance, total cumulative contributions (employee + employer + voluntary), and total dividends earned over the projection horizon.
       
        - ## Malaysian rules encoded
       
        - - **Employee contribution rate:** 11% of basic salary (default; editable for self-employed or special cases)
          - - **Employer contribution rate:** 12% (default; 13% for salaries below RM 5,000) — editable
            - - **i-Saraan / voluntary contribution cap:** RM 100,000 per calendar year, effective Budget 2024 / EPF Act amendment
              - - **Default dividend assumption:** 6% (close to KWSP's historical average) — fully editable to model conservative/optimistic scenarios
               
                - ## Example
               
                - **Scenario:** RM 50,000 current balance, RM 6,000 monthly salary, 25 years to retirement, 6.0% annual dividend, no voluntary contributions.
               
                - **MYkira output:**
                - - Projected balance at retirement: ~**RM 980,000**
                  - - Total contributions (employee + employer): ~RM 414,000
                    - - Total dividends earned: ~**RM 516,000**
