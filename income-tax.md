# Income Tax YA 2025 Calculator

[← Back to MYkira](./)

Compute your Malaysian personal income tax for **Year of Assessment 2025**, with the full set of LHDN reliefs and rebates.

## What it computes

Given:
- **Annual salary income** (employment, EA form)
- - **Other income** (rental, freelance, side income — taxed as ordinary income)
  - - **Marital status** (single / married / married filing separately)
    - - **Reliefs claimed** (see below)
     
      - …MYkira computes:
      - - **Chargeable income** (gross − reliefs)
        - - **Tax before rebates** (per YA 2025 progressive brackets)
          - - **Final tax payable** after rebates (zakat, etc.)
            - - **Effective tax rate**
             
              - ## YA 2025 brackets encoded
             
              - Malaysian resident progressive brackets (Year of Assessment 2025):
             
              - | Chargeable income (RM) | Rate |
              - |---|---|
              - | 0 – 5,000 | 0% |
              - | 5,001 – 20,000 | 1% |
              - | 20,001 – 35,000 | 3% |
              - | 35,001 – 50,000 | 6% |
              - | 50,001 – 70,000 | 11% |
              - | 70,001 – 100,000 | 19% |
              - | 100,001 – 400,000 | 25% |
              - | 400,001 – 600,000 | 26% |
              - | 600,001 – 2,000,000 | 28% |
              - | Above 2,000,000 | 30% |
             
              - Self-employed and business income use the same bracket structure but enter via different fields in MYkira.
             
              - ## Reliefs supported
             
              - MYkira covers the full LHDN relief catalogue for YA 2025:
             
              - - **Self & dependent** — RM 9,000 (auto-applied)
                - - **EPF / KWSP contribution** — up to RM 4,000
                  - - **SOCSO** — up to RM 350
                    - - **Life insurance** — up to RM 3,000
                      - - **Lifestyle relief** (books, electronics, internet, sports) — up to RM 2,500
                        - - **Parents' medical** — up to RM 8,000
                          - - **Children's education** — up to RM 2,000 each
                            - - **Disabled (OKU)** — up to RM 6,000 (self) / 5,000 (spouse) / 6,000 (child)
                              - - **EV charging facilities** — up to RM 2,500 (Budget 2024 incentive)
                                - - **Home loan interest** (specific eligibility window) — up to RM 10,000
                                  - - **PRS / Private Retirement Scheme** — up to RM 3,000
                                    - - **Zakat rebate** — fully offsets tax payable up to amount paid
                                     
                                      - ## Example
                                     
                                      - **Scenario:** Single, RM 72,000 annual salary, RM 4,000 KWSP contribution, RM 2,500 lifestyle, RM 350 SOCSO.
                                     
                                      - **MYkira output:**
                                      - - Gross income: RM 72,000
                                        - - Total reliefs: RM 15,850 (self 9,000 + KWSP 4,000 + lifestyle 2,500 + SOCSO 350)
                                          - - Chargeable income: **RM 56,150**
                                            - - Tax payable (YA 2025 brackets): ~**RM 3,176**
                                              - - Effective tax rate: ~**4.4%**
