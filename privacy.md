# MYkira Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: 9 May 2026**

## 1. Introduction

MYkira ("the app") is a personal finance calculator published by MYkira Kira Resources (a Malaysian sole proprietorship registered with SSM, registration number 202603118665 / AS0514522-T). This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the MYkira iOS and Android apps.

**TL;DR — No data is collected.** MYkira runs entirely on your device. There are no servers, no accounts, and no transmission of any of your financial data anywhere.

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect

MYkira is built as a fully offline calculator. We do not collect, store, or transmit:

- Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, IC number, tax ID)
- - Financial information you enter (income, savings, loan amounts, EPF balances)
  - - Device identifiers (advertising ID, device fingerprint)
    - - Location data (precise or approximate)
      - - Usage analytics or behavioral data
        - - Crash reports containing personal data
         
          - All calculations are performed locally on your iPhone, iPad or Android device. The numbers you enter never leave your device.
         
          - ## 3. Data That Stays on Your Device
         
          - The following is stored locally on your device only, using each platform's standard preferences API (`UserDefaults` on iOS, `SharedPreferences` on Android):
         
          - - Your current language preference (BM / EN / 中文)
            - - Your theme preference (light / dark)
              - - Premium subscription unlock status (a single boolean flag)
               
                - This data never syncs to the cloud, never leaves your device, and is removed automatically when you uninstall the app.
               
                - ## 4. In-App Purchases
               
                - MYkira uses each platform's standard billing framework (StoreKit on iOS, Google Play Billing on Android) for subscriptions and one-time purchases. When you make a purchase:
               
                - - Apple or Google, not MYkira, processes your payment.
                  - - Your payment information is governed by Apple's or Google's privacy policy.
                    - - MYkira receives only an anonymized purchase receipt to unlock premium features on your device.
                      - - The developer does not receive your credit card number, name, billing address, or any personally identifiable purchase information.
                       
                        - ## 5. Third-Party Services
                       
                        - MYkira does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs.
                       
                        - The app uses only first-party platform frameworks:
                       
                        - - **StoreKit** (iOS) / **Google Play Billing** (Android) — for in-app purchases
                          - - **UserDefaults** (iOS) / **SharedPreferences** (Android) — for local settings storage
                           
                            - ## 6. Children's Privacy
                           
                            - MYkira does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13. Because no data is collected at all, the app is safe for use by any age, though its content (tax, loan, EPF calculations) is intended for adult Malaysian residents.
                           
                            - ## 7. Your Rights Under Malaysian PDPA 2010
                           
                            - The Personal Data Protection Act 2010 of Malaysia grants you rights to access, correct, and delete personal data held by service providers. Because MYkira holds no personal data about you, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored preferences are removed by the operating system automatically.
                           
                            - ## 8. International Users
                           
                            - MYkira is distributed only in Malaysia. If you install it elsewhere, the calculations (based on Malaysian tax brackets, KWSP rules, Hire Purchase Act 2026) will not apply to your jurisdiction.
                           
                            - ## 9. Changes to This Policy
                           
                            - This Privacy Policy may be updated if practices change (e.g., if cloud features are added in the future). Material changes will be announced in-app and on this page, with the "Last Updated" date revised.
