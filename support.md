# MYkira — Support

**Last updated: 9 May 2026**

MYkira is the Malaysian personal finance calculator — Compound Interest, KWSP/EPF, Home Loan, Car Loan (with HPAA 2026 EIR toggle), and Income Tax (YA 2025 brackets and reliefs). 100% offline, zero data collection, no ads.

## Common questions

### How do I unlock premium features?

Tap any of the **KWSP**, **Home Loan**, **Car Loan**, or **Income Tax** tabs and follow the in-app paywall to subscribe. Three plans are available:

- **Monthly Premium** — RM 7.90 / month
- - **Yearly Premium** — RM 79.90 / year
  - - **Lifetime Premium** — RM 399.90 (one-time)
   
    - Premium unlock works across all your devices signed into the same Apple ID (iOS) or Google account (Android).
   
    - ### How do I restore a previous purchase?
   
    - In the app: **Settings → Subscription → Restore Purchases**. The app will check with the App Store / Play Store for your active subscriptions or one-time unlocks.
   
    - ### My subscription isn't activating after purchase
   
    - The app waits up to 30 seconds for confirmation from Apple / Google. If it times out:
   
    - 1. Force-quit the app and reopen
      2. 2. Tap **Settings → Restore Purchases**
         3. 3. If still missing, email mykirakiraapp@gmail.com with your purchase receipt
           
            4. ### How are calculations performed?
           
            5. All calculations run locally on your device using Malaysian-specific rules:
           
            6. - **Tax**: YA 2025 personal income tax brackets (resident salaried + self-employed), full relief categories (KWSP, SOCSO, lifestyle, parents, children, OKU, EV charging, home loan interest, zakat rebate, etc.)
               - - **KWSP/EPF**: editable contribution and dividend rates, RM 100,000 voluntary contribution cap (i-Saraan)
                 - - **Home loan**: monthly amortisation, total interest, loan-to-value ratio
                   - - **Car loan**: switch between Hire Purchase Amendment Act 2026 reducing-balance (EIR) and the legacy flat-rate method
                     - - **Compound interest**: principal + regular contributions, multiple compounding frequencies, day/week/month/year investment periods
                      
                       - ### Is my data safe?
                      
                       - **Yes — your numbers never leave your device.** MYkira has no servers, no accounts, no analytics, no advertising SDKs. Inputs are not stored beyond the current calculation session unless you explicitly save them. See the [Privacy Policy](privacy.md) for details.
                      
                       - ### Can I get a refund?
                      
                       - Refund handling is done by the platform:
                      
                       - - **iOS**: report a problem at [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com)
                         - - **Android**: visit [play.google.com](https://play.google.com) → Account → Order history → Request refund
                          
                           - Apple and Google control refund decisions. The developer does not receive payment information and cannot process refunds directly.
                          
                           - ### What languages are supported?
                          
                           - Three: **Bahasa Malaysia**, **English**, and **简体中文**. Switch in **Settings → Language**.
                          
                           - ### I have a feature request or found a bug
                          
                           - Email **mykirakiraapp@gmail.com** with:
                          
                           - - A short description of what you expected vs what happened
                             - - The calculator screen where it occurred
                               - - Your iOS / Android version (helpful but not required)
