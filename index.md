  # MYkira

  **Malaysian finance calculators that respect your privacy.**

  MYkira is a personal finance calculator built for Malaysians. It computes compound interest, KWSP/EPF projections, home loans, car loans (with the new Hire Purchase Amendment Act 2026 reducing-balance toggle), and personal income tax — all using actual Malaysian rules, in Bahasa Malaysia, English, or 中文.

  **100% offline. Zero data collection. No ads. No accounts.**

  The numbers you enter never leave your phone.

  ---

  ## What MYkira does

  - **[Compound Interest](compound-interest/)** — model savings or investment growth with regular contributions, multiple compounding frequencies, and investment periods in days, weeks, months, or years.
  - - **[KWSP / EPF Projection](kwsp/)** — project your retirement balance with editable contribution and dividend rates, including the RM 100,000 voluntary contribution (i-Saraan) cap.
    - - **[Home Loan](home-loan/)** — monthly amortization, total interest, and auto-computed loan-to-value ratio.
      - - **[Car Loan](car-loan/)** — switch between the Hire Purchase Amendment Act 2026 reducing-balance (EIR) method and the legacy flat-rate method to see the real savings.
        - - **[Income Tax YA 2025](income-tax/)** — Malaysian resident salaried + self-employed brackets, with the full set of reliefs (KWSP, SOCSO, lifestyle, parents, children, OKU, EV charging, home loan interest, zakat rebate, and more).
