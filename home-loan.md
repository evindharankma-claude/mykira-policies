# Home Loan Calculator

[← Back to MYkira](./)

Calculate your Malaysian home loan instalment, total interest paid, and loan-to-value ratio with a full amortization schedule.

## What it computes

Given:
- **Property price** (RM)
- - **Down payment** (RM or % — auto-computes the inverse)
  - - **Annual interest rate** (% per year, fixed or assumed-fixed)
    - - **Loan tenure** (years)
     
      - …MYkira computes:
      - - **Monthly instalment**
        - - **Loan amount** (price − down payment)
          - - **Loan-to-value ratio** (loan / price)
            - - **Total interest paid** over the tenure
              - - **Year-by-year amortization** showing principal vs interest split
               
                - ## Why loan-to-value matters in Malaysia
               
                - Bank Negara's responsible lending guidelines and most Malaysian banks (Maybank, CIMB, Public Bank, RHB) cap home loans at 90% LTV for first-time buyers and tighter for second/third properties. MYkira computes LTV automatically so you see at a glance whether your down payment puts you in eligible territory or whether you need to negotiate a higher down payment.
               
                - ## Example
               
                - **Scenario:** RM 500,000 property, RM 50,000 down payment (10%), 4.0% annual interest, 30-year tenure.
               
                - **MYkira output:**
                - - Loan amount: **RM 450,000**
                  - - LTV: **90%**
                    - - Monthly instalment: ~**RM 2,148**
                      - - Total interest over 30 years: ~**RM 323,400**
